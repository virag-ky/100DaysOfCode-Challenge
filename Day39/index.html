Position Overview:

We are seeking a talented and creative Product Designer to join our team. As a Product Designer at Wisely, you will play a pivotal role in shaping the user experience and visual identity of our products. You will work closely with cross-functional teams including product managers, engineers, and marketers to create intuitive and visually stunning solutions that delight our customers.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with product managers and engineers to define product requirements and develop user-centric design solutions.
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and mockups to effectively communicate design ideas and concepts.
  • Develop user interface designs that are intuitive, user-friendly, and visually appealing.
  • Conduct user research and gather feedback to iteratively improve product designs.
  • Ensure consistency and coherence across all product touchpoints by maintaining design systems and style guides.
  • Stay updated on industry trends and best practices to continuously enhance design processes and methodologies.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience as a Product Designer or similar role, with a strong portfolio showcasing your design expertise.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Sketch, Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, etc.
  • Solid understanding of user-centered design principles and methodologies.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills with the ability to articulate and defend design decisions.
  • Strong attention to detail and a passion for creating exceptional user experiences.
  • Experience working in an Agile development environment is a plus.
